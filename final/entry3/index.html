experts

My sister, who works as a graphic designer, as well as a cousin who works in a similar industry, were both shown my current game on. They have been in the real industry for many years than I have even been in college, so I felt that they were qualified to give me advice. I asked them, what could be added to this project, aside from an overhaul of code, that would make it more engaging and visually interesting?

critique

They mentioned that my art is easily the strongest part, and to keep in the same direction for any other elements that would be added. My sister stressed that the current animation style is something to maintain through the final project. They all mentioned that readability was an issue, and could be helped with a darker, different background. On top of that, they agreed that some type of transition, whether a fade in/cut out/slide in would help tie in the project, especially since I told them that I planned on adding a bit more to the project. For example, they said that the map I would add could be animated when hovered or clicked on, ideally with visual indicators. They mentioned the Pok√©mon in game map as a reference point. Sound was also a suggestion, as sound could really help tie in the animations seen throughout the game, as well as establishing setting more effectively.

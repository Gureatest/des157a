plans

In this project, I plan to add a lot of visualization and interactivity to the gameOn project, particularly with more art and more functions. For example, I drew out a level map and a different battle scene. After speaking with experts, I also decided to focus on sound and transitions, so those will hopefully be added as well. A lot of inspiration will be from the elements of other similar games, where the main goal is to keep the user captivated and interested.

date: october 19th, 2023

I think that usability should be a stronger focus for web and app designers. Even looking at the UC Davis Parking app, AMP Park, has awful UI, is basically unusable, and the ratings on the app page rave about how unusable and awful it is. A frustrating design can literally make or break a design and prevent a user from interacting with it at all depending on how patient they are. At a certain point it becomes less about speed and more about willingness to participate.
